General

I am a third-year PhD student in the Vanderbilt Department of Mathematics under the supervision of Professor Marcelo Disconzi. I received my Bachelors of Science degrees from Purdue University in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering and Mathematics. My research interests are in partial differential equations and fluid dynamics. Currently, I am a member of the Vanderbilt PDE Research Group and the Vanderbilt Initiative for Gravity, Waves, and Fluids . Here is my CV and Google Scholar.
